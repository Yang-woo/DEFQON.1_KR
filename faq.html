Can I attend Defqon.1 with a wheelchair or disability?휠체어를 이용하거나 장애가 있어도 데프콘에 참석할 수 있나요?

Yes, of course! Below you can find all the information you need.

Parking for visitors with disabilities

Visitors in possession of a valid Parking Card for People with Disabilities (also known as the European Disability Parking Card or, in Dutch: Gehandicaptenparkeerkaart / GPK) may request access to the designated accessible parking spaces at the event.

To do so, please send an email in advance with your request to travel@q-dance.com. In your email, please include a photo of your valid blue disability parking card (GPK) and the type of entry ticket you purchased.

We will review your application and provide further information regarding the procedure and confirmation. Requests must be submitted no later than May 15, 2026.

Please note: This facility is only available to holders of the official blue European Disability Parking Card (GPK). If you do not have this card, it is unfortunately not possible to park in these spaces, and any submitted requests will not be processed. The official blue European Disability Parking Card (GPK) will be verified on-site. Any misuse of the card will be reported.

Entering the festival

All entrances feature one-meter-wide gates for easy access. Extra-wide gates are clearly marked and available across the different entrance areas.

Festival grounds

Accessible toilets are available at every toilet area across the festival site. The premium decks can be accessed via a ramp or lift; however, a Premium Ticket is required for entry.

Wheelchair rental

We do not offer wheelchair rentals at Defqon.1, so please bring your own if needed.

Camping grounds

Planning to stay with us at the campsite? That is possible!

Would you like to stay in a pre-set-up tent at The Highlands or The Sanctuary? Please contact travel@q-dance.com with your request and the type of accommodation you would like to book or have already booked. We will do our best to help you find a suitable solution.

If you are bringing your own camping gear or have other questions, please contact travel@q-dance.com for more information. Requests regarding accessible accommodations or camping spots must be submitted no later than May 15, 2026.
